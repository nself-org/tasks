## Description (4000 chars max)
ɳTask is a privacy-first task manager that connects to your own self-hosted backend — no subscriptions, no vendor lock-in, your data always under your control.

**Your data, your server**
ɳTask works with any nself-powered backend. Set it up in 5 minutes on your own VPS and own your data completely. No cloud accounts required.

**Works with multiple backends**
Connect to nself (self-hosted), Supabase, or Nhost — whichever you prefer. Switch at any time without losing data.

**Native iOS features**
- Face ID / Touch ID authentication
- Home Screen widget to see your tasks at a glance
- Share extension to capture tasks from any app
- Push notifications for reminders and due dates
- Haptic feedback throughout

**Built for productivity**
- Unlimited lists and tasks
- Due dates and reminders
- Task descriptions and attachments
- Real-time sync across all devices
- Works offline — syncs when back online

**Open source**
ɳTask is MIT-licensed and fully open source. Inspect the code, self-host the backend, and build your own integrations.
